Come for the food, stay for the view!

Located on New Harbor

Dead Eye Dick’s at Payne’s Dock in New Harbor is open for lunch and dinner daily. Dead Eye’s eclectic menu offers the freshest seafood, including Dead Eye’s signature swordfish and lobster dishes. Open from May through September, diners can enjoy homemade soups, an extensive raw bar, creative cocktails, wine and microbrews in our casual dining room or outside on the deck overlooking New Harbor.

Open 7 days a week

Lunch from 11:30am to 3:00pm

Dinner from 5:00pm to Close

Known for our fresh seafood, we offer a variety of delicious seafood dishes and also offer amazing non-seafood dishes. Along with our numerous dishes we curate a new cocktail, beer, and wine list every season. Looking to incorporate fresh ingredients and local provisions whenever we can!
